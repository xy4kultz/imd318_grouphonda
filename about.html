THE FOUNDER OF Honda

ONE OF THE WORLD'S MIGHTIEST MOTOR CORPORATIONS.

Mr. Soichiro Honda was born in Hamamatsu, in the Shizuoka Prefecture of Japan, in 1906. His father owned a blacksmith’s shop and repaired bicycles as a side-line. As a teenager, Soichiro Honda was apprenticed to a car repair shop in Tokyo, but business was slack as there were few cars in the city at that time. However, this changed after the devastation of the 1923 earthquake. The Japanese government decided to invest in mechanised mass transit. To his luck, Mr. Honda found his services in heavy demand.

Our History

Soichiro Honda and Takeo Fujisawa formed Honda in 1948, specializing on motorcycles at first before branching out into cars.

With the classic Honda Cub being one of the best-selling cars ever, the business swiftly rose to prominence in the motorbike market worldwide.

Honda's breakthrough in the automotive industry began in 1963 when it introduced the T360 truck and the S500 sports car.

Honda gained recognition throughout the years for its dependable, fuel-efficient vehicles, such as the Civic and Accord, as well as its advancements in racing and engine technology.

Honda is a leader in electric and hybrid cars today, carrying on its tradition of sustainability and innovation.

Honda Advanced Technology

Honda continues to lead the automotive industry with cutting-edge technology designed to make every drive safer, more efficient, and more enjoyable. Our advanced technologies are integrated into every model we produce, ensuring that you experience the best of innovation. Below are some of the key advanced technologies developed by Honda:

Honda Sensing®

Honda Sensing® is a suite of safety and driver-assist technologies designed to enhance the driver's situational awareness and, in some cases, intervene to avoid collisions or lessen their severity. It includes features like Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC), Collision Mitigation Braking System (CMBS™), Lane Keeping Assist System (LKAS), and more.

Earth Dreams Technology

Earth Dreams Technology is Honda’s approach to fuel-efficient, high-performance engines. This innovative technology reduces fuel consumption and emissions, while increasing engine performance. By applying advanced engineering principles, Honda has created a more eco-friendly solution without sacrificing performance.

e:HEV (Hybrid Electric Vehicle) Technology

Honda's e:HEV (Hybrid Electric Vehicle) technology is designed to reduce emissions and improve fuel efficiency. This system combines a high-output electric motor with an internal combustion engine to create a seamless driving experience with better fuel economy and fewer carbon emissions.

VTEC® Engine Technology

VTEC® (Variable Valve Timing and Lift Electronic Control) technology allows Honda engines to adapt to driving conditions, providing greater fuel efficiency at low speeds and enhanced performance at higher speeds. This innovative technology has been a key factor in Honda’s success in both road cars and motorsport.

Advanced Connectivity

Honda integrates advanced connectivity features like Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ into its vehicles, making it easier for drivers to stay connected while on the road. With the ability to connect your smartphone to your car’s infotainment system, you can enjoy a range of apps, navigation, and hands-free calling.
